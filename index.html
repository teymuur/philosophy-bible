Philosophers Timeline

470-399 BCE

Socrates

Founder of Western philosophy, known for Socratic method

428-348 BCE

Plato

Founder of Academy, Theory of Forms

384-322 BCE

Aristotle

Logic, metaphysics, ethics, natural sciences

1596-1650

René Descartes

Father of modern philosophy, "I think, therefore I am"

1724-1804

Immanuel Kant

Critique of Pure Reason, transcendental idealism

1844-1900

Friedrich Nietzsche

Will to power, eternal recurrence, übermensch

1889-1976

Martin Heidegger

Being and Time, fundamental ontology

1905-1980

Jean-Paul Sartre

Existence precedes essence, radical freedom

1926-1984

Michel Foucault

Power relations, discourse analysis

1925-1995

Gilles Deleuze

Difference and repetition, rhizome theory

1930-2004

Jacques Derrida

Deconstruction, différance
